General Interview Tips

  • Research the company and role thoroughly before the interview
  • Prepare your portfolio and be ready to discuss specific projects
  • Practice your answers to common questions
  • Prepare questions to ask the interviewer
  • Dress appropriately for the company culture

Answering Behavioral Questions

STAR Method

  • Situation: Describe the context and background
  • Task: Explain the challenge or goal
  • Action: Detail the steps you took
  • Result: Share the outcomes and learnings

Portfolio Presentation Tips

  • Focus on your process, not just the final result
  • Highlight your role and contributions
  • Include both successes and challenges
  • Be ready to discuss design decisions
  • Show your problem-solving approach

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Being too vague in your answers
  • Not providing specific examples
  • Focusing too much on tools rather than process
  • Not asking questions about the role/company
  • Being unprepared for technical questions

Questions to Ask Interviewers

  • What does success look like in this role?
  • How does the design team collaborate with other departments?
  • What are the biggest challenges the team is facing?
  • How does the company support professional development?
  • What's the design process like at the company?
